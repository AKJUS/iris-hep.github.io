    project_goal: >
        For the HL-LHC CMS experiment, the Fast Beam Condition Monitor (FBCM), a silicon pad detector with a fast ASIC, is being developed. Although FBCM replacement is planned after half of Phase 2, possible maintenance delays make it essential to understand the performance, charge collection efficiency, and reliability of sensors operating beyond the expected radiation dose.
