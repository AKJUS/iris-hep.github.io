## My research:
I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Physics at Temple University working with the STAR Collaboration at RHIC, Brookhaven National Laboratory. My research focuses on the spin structure of the proton through measurements of transverse spin asymmetries in polarized proton-proton collisions. I study di-hadron production, including pion-kaon and kaon-kaon pairs, to investigate transversity and interference fragmentation functions.

## My expertise is:
High-energy nuclear and particle physics
Data analysis with ROOT and C++
Statistical data analysis
Particle identification using the STAR TPC and TOF detectors
Scientific computing on Linux-based HPC systems
