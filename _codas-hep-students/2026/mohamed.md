## My research:
I study neutrino interactions in MicroBooNE, focusing on charged-current single-$\pi^0$ production and transverse kinematic imbalance measurements.

## My expertise is:
Neutrino cross-section analysis, LArTPC reconstruction, C++, Python and ROOT.

## A problem I'm grappling with:
Understanding how nuclear effects and final-state interactions shape neutrino-argon measurements.
## I've got my eyes on:
Improving neutrino-interaction modeling for current and future LArTPC experiments.

## I want to know more about:
Modern software, scalable computing, and machine-learning tools for high-energy physics analyses.
